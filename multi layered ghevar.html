Multi-layered Ghevar

    Ingredients Of Multi-Layered Ghevar
  • 1 3/4 Cup maida
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1/4 cup ghee (cooled), melted

    • For sugar syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • arrowroot
    How to Make Multi-layered Ghevar
    Prepare sugar syrup:
  1. Combine the sugar and water in a pan and simmer till it reaches a 1 string consistency.
  2. Remove from the heat and keep warm.
    Prepare Ghevar:
  • Combine the flour, arrowroot and melted ghee in a bowl.
  • Add 1 cup of water, whisking continuously Add 2 more cups of water again in a thin stream while whisking continuously
  • Keep the batter in a cool place away from the heat.
  • Place the ghevar ring in a kadhai and pour melted ghee in it till it reaches 3/4 of the height of the mould.
  • Heat the ghee on a medium flame and put in one spoonful of the batter into the mould in a thin stream. The batter should settle in the mould.
  • Repeat seven times making a hole in the centre of the ghevar using a wooden skewer stick. Pour the batter into this centre each time.
  • Once cooked take out and Immerse in sugar syrup, drain quickly and place on a serving plate.
  • Arrange multiple layers with different sizes and serve.
